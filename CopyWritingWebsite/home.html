Example Copy

Effective ads and sales pages must be specific to target market.

We want to pique curiosity, emotion, or relate to the reader through story.

Imagine an ad for a workout program targeted towards 30-40 year old men that started gaining too much weight for their liking, and then they read this:


This was a MONUMENTAL discovery.

I have been self-conscious about my appearance in the mirror for as long as I can remember.

No matter which diet plan or training routine I tried, I simply could not attain the body I desired.

I would engage in endless cardio and follow miserable diets, yet nothing seemed to make a difference.

But then, I came across the game-changer.

The fat began melting away.

I was actually able to enjoy my meals.

I would spend less than 30 minutes in the gym each day.

I cracked the code for transforming your physique!

Discovery How I Effortlessly Lost Weight and Transformed My Physique


This would be a story that relates to many of the target audience, exposes their pain, and teases the solution.

The power of story, curiosity, and emotion are powerful!
